how this works
single-file idle clicker with too many mechanics. ☰ menu has tabs.

tap 🍞 to collect. spend in the treats shop on bakers (passive rate) and tap upgrades. tap value scales with your passive rate.

combo — fast taps (10 / 25 / 50 in a row) give ×2 / ×3 / ×5 bonuses. crit — 5% of taps deal ×10 with a gold ✦.

chaos events every 30-90s. live buffs/debuffs show as pills under the rate. 3+ buffs in a row → next is doubled.

golden 🥯 spawns ~once a minute. tap within 9s for 3 minutes of rate plus a random buff (frenzy ×7, lucky ×3, etc).

sourdough phases 🥖 cycle every 30min — knead/rise/bake/cool/stale, each rewires global rules. countdown is in the header.

achievements 🏆 — each adds +1% rate. stats live in the same tab.

ascend 👑 at 1B lifetime for crowns (sqrt L/1B). each crown +5% rate forever. spend in 🪙 shop on permanent upgrades.

crumb trail 🍞 — a ripe crumb appears every hour real-time. spend on the pantheon.

yeast garden 🌱 — 3×3 plots. plant 6 species (sourdough, hops, barley, tinyclaw, stardust, voidmold). real-time growth, neighbor pollination.

pantheon 🏛️ (post-ascension) — slot 3 of 6 temperaments in heart/mind/belly slots. every temperament has a tradeoff.

yeast market 📈 (post-1T lifetime) — 5 commodities, prices fluctuate. own a baker → its commodity runs hot.

burnt crusts 🥵 (post-1B) — pests attach to bakers, drain rate. tap to pop for 110% of accumulated drain.

bakery chips at the bottom of the play area: tap = info, long-press = sell 1 for 50% refund.
