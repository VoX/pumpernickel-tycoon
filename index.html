how this works
single-file idle clicker. tap, buy, ascend, repeat.

tap 🍞 to collect pumpernickels. spend them in the treats shop on bakers (passive rate) and tap upgrades.

combo — keep tapping fast (10 / 25 / 50 in a row) for ×2 / ×3 / ×5 streak bonuses.

crit — 5% of taps deal ×10 damage, with a gold sparkle ✦ float.

chaos events fire every 30-90s. buffs (green border), debuffs (red), instant gains/losses (amber). live effects show as pills under the rate. 3+ buffs in a row → next buff is doubled.

golden 🥯 spawns once a minute or so. tap it within 9 seconds for 60 seconds of your current rate as instant bonus.

achievements 🏆 — each one earned adds +1% rate. some are hidden.

ascend 👑 at 1B lifetime. trade your kingdom for crowns (sqrt of lifetime / 1B). each crown grants +5% rate forever. spend crowns in the 🪙 shop on permanent meta-upgrades.
